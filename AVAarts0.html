About Us

AVA Arts is an online platform created to help beginners and art enthusiasts learn and explore different forms of art. The website provides useful information about drawing, painting, and creative techniques in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

Art Styles

Art styles are distinct visual languages characterized by specific techniques, aesthetics, and themes, often evolving from artists' personal interpretations, influences, and deviations from reality. Major styles range from traditional realism to abstract, surrealist, and contemporary forms, each offering unique ways of rendering light, color, and form.

Art Supplies

Essential art supplies for artists include quality paints (acrylics, watercolors, oils), diverse brushes, sketchbooks, pencils, erasers, and surfaces like canvas or thick paper. Beginners should invest in artist-quality paints and brushes for better results. Common tools also include palette knives, gesso for priming, and easels for support.

Top 3 famous paintings

Mona Lisa

The Mona Lisa is a world-renowned half-length portrait by Leonardo da Vinci depicting a seated woman, likely Lisa del Giocondo, against a distant, hazy landscape. Painted in oil on a poplar wood panel, it is celebrated for its sfumato technique, enigmatic smile, and serene expression.
